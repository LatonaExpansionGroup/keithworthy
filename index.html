Bio

Keith Worthy & Aesthetic Audio: The Authentic Soul of Detroit. As the visionary founder of Aesthetic Audio and a deeply respected pillar of the Detroit underground, Keith Worthy represents the uncompromising, purist lineage of electronic music. Through Aesthetic Audio, Worthy has cultivated a legendary status within the global vinyl community, bypassing fleeting commercial trends to champion timeless, audiophile-grade artistry. The label stands as a premier destination for serious record collectors and a testament to his dedication to sonic purity.

Standing shoulder-to-shoulder with legendary peers like Rick Wade and Patrice Scott, he is a guardian of the city's rich musical heritage.
